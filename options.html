Edge-Touch Pager

Edge-touch pager lets you scroll through web pages by touching the edges of your screen, just like an ebook reader.

Edge-touch pager is enabled.

Using edge-touch pager

Tap the right edge of the screen to Scroll down by one screenful. Tap the left edge to scroll up.

Swipe up on either edge of the screen to scroll part of the page to the top of the screen. Swipe down to move it to the bottom.

Press and hold to click on parts of the page near the edges of the screen instead of scrolling.

Advanced settings

Edge-touch area width

Use the slider below to adjust the width of the touch-sensitive area on either side of the screen.

Under-scroll

Use the slider below to adjust how much of the previous screenful to keep visible after scrolling. This can help make finding your place again easier.

Long-press duration

Set the duration required for presses to click on parts of a web page in the edge zones.

Smooth scrolling

Choose whether to animate scrolling actions or not. For best results on eInk devices, disable smooth scrolling.
